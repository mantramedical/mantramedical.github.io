Our other concepts

Mantra Medical has a range of further concepts that are not yet in active development due to budgetary restrictions. These include:

  1. An improved design of pulse oximeter for use on very young children.
  2. A moving and handling device to assist ambulance crews in lifting patients on spinal boards.
  3. A new device to revolutionise cervical spine protection in trauma patients, ensuring greater stabilisation but also greater safety.
  4. An adaptor to ensure continuous delivery of nebulised drugs regardless of patient position.

With investment we would seek to place all concepts into full development in accordance with a detailed financial plan. With this range of concepts we feel that Mantra Medical has the potential to establish itself across several sections of the medical market,increasing our presence and the likelihood of a return.
