About us

Founded by a medical doctor, Mantra Medical Ltd is developing a range of products that address fundamental problems in healthcare. Focusing initially on targeted aspects of infection control, the problems we address are universal, neglected, costly and critical to patient safety.

Our foundational mantra is infection prevention for safer healthcare. Guided by the medical insight of the company founder, our products will fit seamlessly within existing workflow pathways, overcoming barriers to compliance and driving improvements in infection control practice across healthcare.

Mantra Medical is a growing, pre-market enterprise. We have successfully raised several rounds of investment and work with a range of advisors and mentors to realise our ambition. We recently won a place on the 2019/20 Y-Accelerator program and the company founder is a fellow on the NHS Clinical Entrepreneur Programme.

Our team

Our 3 mantras

The following core ideas are central to everything Mantra Medical does:

  1. Infection prevention for safer healthcare

  2. Integrate into existing staff workflow

  3. Ensure everyone benefits

We aim to provide solutions that allow compliance with desired behaviour to be easier than non-compliance. Achieving this will require innovative solutions that draw upon a deep understanding of the healthcare environment.
