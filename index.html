Our innovations

  1. Mantra One - Pulse oximeter cleaner

    European patent number: 2595561

    The Mantra One™, our patented pulse oximeter cleaning device, provides consistent and routine cleaning of pulse oximeters at the bedside without adding to staff workload.

  2. Mantra Two - Blood pressure cuff cleaner

    European patent number: 2629805

    The Mantra Two is our patented blood pressure cuff cleaning device. To the user, the device is simply a convenient place to put the blood pressure cuff after use, whilst cleaning is done passively without interaction.
